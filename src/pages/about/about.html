About

Arya Kshatriya Yuva Samithi (AKYS) is an Association of ARYA KSHATIRYA YOUTH who formed together for Welfare and Development of ARYA KSHATRIYA COMMUNITY! It’s a state wide common platform for ARYA KSHATRIYA/ ARE / AREVALLU to share their ideas and do service for the development of the community and society.

Moto: To guide the youth regarding Education, Employment. And to develop oneness among the community by conducting various events.

Core Objectives:

To guide our community youth regarding Educational Opportunities. Will make aware of various educational fields and its future aspects

To guide our Community people in the Getting Employment. Will inform about various employment and business opportunities’ available in the market as per their qualifications and experience.

To build an active support system between our community youth and other state wide registered community wings. It’s a platform to pass all necessary information of our community and to share your young views for our development. It’s a bridge between key people in our community and younger generation. "AKYS" is a hand on hand Supportive chain System to "UNITE OUR COMMUNITY" by bringing all under one Umbrella for overall development of the community in every aspects.

Finally,AKYS will work on Education, Employement, Enlightement, Eentertainments and Events
